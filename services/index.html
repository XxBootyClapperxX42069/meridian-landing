What We Do

A cleaner sourcing process from supplier search to cost review.

Every engagement starts with a clear scope. You tell us what you need to source, where you are trying to land it, your target quantity, and what a good outcome looks like. From there, Meridian builds the process around supplier discovery, quote control, risk review, and practical cost visibility.

Find

Supplier Discovery

We identify potential manufacturers, exporters, and specialist suppliers across Chinese and Southeast Asian manufacturing hubs.

  • Supplier registration review
  • Factory versus trading company positioning
  • Product category fit
  • Export experience
  • MOQ suitability
  • Sample availability
  • Documentation readiness
  • Commercial responsiveness

Compare

RFQ Structuring & Quote Comparison

Good sourcing depends on comparable quotes. Meridian structures RFQs so suppliers are quoting against the same product assumptions, quantities, packaging requirements, and commercial terms.

  • Unit price
  • MOQ tiers
  • Lead time
  • Sample cost
  • Payment terms
  • Incoterms
  • Packaging assumptions
  • Supplier notes and risk flags

Cost

Landed-Cost Visibility

The cheapest unit price is rarely the real cost. Meridian helps buyers estimate landed-cost assumptions by reviewing freight options, duty considerations, order volume, packaging, documentation, inspection, and other hidden cost factors.

  • FOB / EXW / CIF comparison
  • Freight method comparison
  • Duty and tariff assumptions
  • Packaging and labelling cost flags
  • Inspection or testing cost flags
  • Per-unit cost modelling by order size

Verify

Supplier & Documentation Review

Meridian helps organize supplier risk notes and documentation review before a buyer commits to larger orders. Buyers decide what requires third-party verification, legal review, customs review, lab testing, or inspection.

  • Business identity checks
  • Certification document review
  • Issuer database checks where available
  • COA / specification document organization
  • Pre-shipment inspection coordination
  • Factory audit coordination
  • Supplier risk scoring

Buy

Sample & Supply Coordination

For buyers who want support beyond research, Meridian can help coordinate sample requests, supplier communication, quote revisions, freight-forwarder introductions, and repeat-order benchmarking.

  • Sample request coordination
  • Quote revision support
  • Supplier communication
  • Freight-forwarder introduction
  • Alternative supplier benchmarking
  • Repeat-order support
  • Basic consolidation and repack coordination by review

Important

Compliance-aware sourcing language.

Meridian does not provide legal, medical, customs, or regulatory advice. Buyers are responsible for final compliance review before import, marketing, resale, or use.
