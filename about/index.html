About Meridian

We work on the buyer's side.

Meridian Group Trade was founded to solve a specific sourcing problem: most buyers know what they want to source, but not how to confirm who actually makes it, what it should cost to land, or which supplier terms should be taken seriously.

Not a marketplace.

We do not list suppliers and collect referral fees. We take a sourcing brief, build the research, organize supplier responses, and deliver something actionable: a shortlist you can review, a quote set you can compare, and a cost picture that reflects reality before you commit capital.

Our sourcing work spans consumer goods, wellness products, specialty ingredients, packaging, hardware, and repeatable B2B supply needs. We focus heavily on China-based manufacturing because that is where many factories, exporters, and specialist suppliers operate — and because doing it right requires more than searching a directory.

The approach.

Every engagement is scoped. You pay for the work, not access to a supplier list.

Meridian operates on a buyer-side procurement model. The objective is to help the buyer understand their options clearly — not to push a supplier, hide margin in product pricing, or make a weak quote look better than it is.

If the research shows that your current supplier is already the strongest option, we will say that. If a product category requires additional compliance, licensing, testing, customs review, or legal review, we will flag that early so the buyer can decide whether to proceed properly.

  • Supplier identity
  • Factory versus trader position
  • Documentation carefully
  • Quotes on the same basis
  • Claims that need third-party verification
