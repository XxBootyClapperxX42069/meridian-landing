Privacy Policy

How Meridian uses sourcing request information.

Meridian Group Trade collects information submitted through website forms and direct business communication so we can review sourcing requests and respond with next steps.

Information collected

Forms may collect name, company, email, phone, product category, target quantity, destination country, required documentation or certifications, and message or sourcing details.

How RFQ details are used

RFQ and sourcing details are used to understand the buyer request, assess whether Meridian can assist, prepare follow-up questions, and communicate next steps.

No sale of buyer information

Meridian Group Trade does not sell buyer information or publish product details submitted through the website.

Business communication

Submitted contact information may be used for business communication related to the sourcing request, procurement support, follow-up questions, or related service discussions.

Data retention

Information may be retained for business records, request continuity, dispute prevention, and future reference related to the sourcing engagement. Buyers may contact Meridian to ask about their submitted information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact ldunzl@meridiangrouptrade.com.
